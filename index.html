Our Story

A Name New Ulm
Has Trusted for 25 Years

Schultz Plumbing, Heating & Drain Cleaning has kept Brown County homes and businesses running since 1999. What started as one master plumber's promise — do the job right, treat people like neighbors — is still exactly how we work today.

From a dripping faucet to a full bathroom remodel, from a frozen line at 2 a.m. to a new high-efficiency water heater, we bring the same honest, careful workmanship to every call. No upselling, no shortcuts — just plumbing done properly the first time.

What We Do

Full-Service Plumbing & Heating

Service & Repair

Leaks, clogs, faucets, toilets, sump pumps and burst pipes — fast, tidy fixes for any home plumbing problem.

Water Heaters

Repair, replacement and installation of tank & tankless water heaters — hot water back on the same day.

Heating & HVAC

Furnaces, boilers and in-floor radiant heat — keeping New Ulm winters comfortable and efficient.

Drain & Sewer Cleaning

Power drain cleaning and sewer rodding that clears the toughest blockages without the mess.

Septic Service

Septic tank & system cleaning and maintenance to keep everything flowing on rural & in-town properties.

Gas Piping

Licensed natural-gas line installation and repair — one of the few local plumbers certified for the work.

Bathroom Remodeling

Tubs, showers, vanities and fixtures — full bathroom updates handled start to finish by one trusted crew.
